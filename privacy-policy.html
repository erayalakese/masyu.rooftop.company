Privacy Policy

Thank you for playing Masyu Challenge! This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: We do not actively collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, address, email address, or phone number.

Non-Personal Information: We may collect non-personal information, such as:

  • Device information: Type of device, operating system, and hardware settings.
  • Usage data: Game progress, levels completed, time spent playing, and in-game actions.
  • Analytics data: Anonymous usage statistics collected through Firebase Analytics to improve the game.

2. How We Collect Information

Automatically: We collect some information automatically through the use of Firebase Analytics.

User actions: We collect information about your game progress and actions as you play the game.

3. Use of Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the game: We use usage data to understand how players interact with the game and to make improvements.
  • Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to analyze game usage and trends, helping us to optimize the game and provide a better user experience.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share personal information with third parties. Non-personal information may be shared with:

  • Firebase Analytics: For analytics purposes only.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Masyu Challenge does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us.
